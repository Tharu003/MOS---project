Sports Development Goals

Empowering students through discipline, equality, and innovation in sports across the Southern Province.

01

Healthy Generation

Promote a disciplined, healthy, and strong student generation through sports and develop leadership and teamwork.

02

Participation & Recognition

Encourage student participation in sports and recognize achievements to boost motivation and morale.

03

Development Plans

Establish school to provincial-level sports plans, ensure continuous activity, improve infrastructure, and form sports clubs.

04

Equal Opportunities

Identify talented students, ensure equal sports access for all, and implement fair selections and innovative programs.

05

Monitoring & Quality

Monitor performance, evaluate program outcomes, guide physical education, and enhance sports-related educational quality.

Objectives

Strategic goals and initiatives of the Southern Province Sports Department

Operational Principles

Improve participation and awareness in youth development through regional and national activities. Promote value-based and skill-driven engagement.

  • Engage youth in minimum 2 national-level development programs annually.
  • Promote leadership and teamwork through strategic partnerships.

Competitions & Goals

Organize competitions to encourage sports and cultural excellence.

  • Hold 3 divisional-level competitions yearly.
  • Achieve 20% participation in national competitions.
  • Involve at least 5% of total youth in certified activities.

Performance Targets

Initiatives focused on practical community contribution and service.

  • Plant 100+ trees in youth zones.
  • Create 20 community gardens.
  • Establish 7 recycling & eco-waste centers.
  • Organize 14-day service programs for youth groups.

Evaluation & Reporting

Performance evaluation based on activity reports and documentation.

  • Submit complete activity report for 2024.
  • Minimum score of 47 out of 100 for recognition.
  • Top reports rewarded with 5% bonus marks.
